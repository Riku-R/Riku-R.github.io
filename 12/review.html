My review of the game

Having played DOTA 2 for thousands of hours and watched and studied it for many more thousands of hours, I think DOTA 2 is one of the best games that I have come across ever. A wise man once told me, "DOTA 2 is, at the same time, the best and worst game of all time." There will certainly be moments where everyone, who has ever played DOTA 2 will most likely agree.

DOTA 2 at it's core is a very balanced and routinely maintained game with A LOT to offer. Getting better at the game and noticing your progress while winning tough games against other skilled players is a very rewarding and euphoric experience. There is no perfect player or strategy for the game because there is always room for improvement, and that is what drives most players forward.

The problem with having a very addictive game, where losses can be solely attributed to other teammates, is that players very easily start blaming their teammates for not doing their "job" properly. This can create a very hostile and negative atmosphere and bring down the whole team's morale.

My rating for DOTA 2 is a perfect 10/10. It is extremely difficult to get into, but once you do, you get to enjoy one of the best games ever made!

What do other people think?

There are over 1.6 million user reviews for DOTA 2 on Steam, of which, 83% are positive. Most of the negative reviews are pointed towards the negative community and environment surrounding DOTA 2 and not the game itself.
Metacritic's review score for the game is 90/100, -"Simply irresistible and addictive as hell."
